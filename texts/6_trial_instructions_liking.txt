On each screen, you will see three products in a triad. The product on top of the triad is a beef steak. The pair on the bottom are plant-based steaks. 

In the first task you will be asked to indicate which of the two plant-based steaks is more similar to the beef steak. 

If the plant-based steak on the left is most similar, you press X.
If the plant-based steak on the right is most similar, you press Y.

Please focus on the task and answer as fast as you can without thinking too much. We are interested in your initial responses.

Before the main task begins, you will do a couple of practice rounds to get comfortable with the task.

Please click on the arrow to start the practice trials.
