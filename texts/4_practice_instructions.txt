For the following task, we are interested in how you perceive different products. 

On each screen, you will see three products in a triad.

In the first task you will be asked to indicate which of the two products at the bottom of the screen is more similar to the product on the top of the screen. 

If the product on the left is most similar, you press D.

If the product on the right is most similar, you press K.

Please focus on the task and answer as fast as you can without thinking too much. We are interested in your initial responses.

Before the main task begins, you will do a couple of practice rounds to get comfortable with the task.

Please click on the SPACE BAR to start the practice trials.
